Just Survive

As part of the Web Development course at HES-SO Valais, we proudly present Just Survive. You play as a survivor in a post-apocalyptic Sierre, following a devastating virus outbreak (COVID-19). With no rules and no law, your only mission is to stay alive against relentless waves of enemies determined to eliminate you.

How to Win

A timer runs as you fight for survival. Every second you stay alive earns you points. However, be warned: enemies become stronger over time. You have weapons to defend yourself, but your stamina is limited. If you die, the game is over.

Gameplay

  • Choose your avatar and explore the ruined streets of Sierre.
  • Use weapons to eliminate enemies and earn survival points.
  • Collect weapons and health kits to improve your chances of survival.
  • Enemies grow stronger as time passes, making survival increasingly challenging.

Technologies

This game is built using:

  • JavaScript
  • HTML
  • CSS

Inspiration

Just Survive draws inspiration from classic survival and horror games such as Grand Theft Auto (GTA) and Doom.
